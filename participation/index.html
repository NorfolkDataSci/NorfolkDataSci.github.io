    How to Participate

    If you're interested in Data Science, get involved! There are many ways to contribute to the group and you don't even have to be a Data Scientist!

    If you are...

    Just curious

    We're on slack! Join the conversation. This is a great way to meet the people in Norfolk Data Science and learn more about how you can participate. Just send a request to NorfolkDataSci@gmail.com and then check your email for an invite!

    A Social Butterfly

    Checkout our monthly meeting! All you need to do is head over to our MeetUp page and RSVP (and then show up, of course..).

    A Coder

    We are always looking for new projects and new data sets to get our hands dirty with. Checkout our Github Repository and see if anything catches your eye. You're only a fork and pull request away from being a contributor! If you don't see anything you like but have an idea, let us know (NorfolkDataSci@gmail.com) and we'll get it added.

    Interested?

    Do all three! Hit us up on Slack, come to a meeting, and start contributing to or propose a project!
