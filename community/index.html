    Our Mission

    The mission of Norfolk Data Science is to teach, motivate, mobilize, and empower others to create a community that respects the power of data science. Norfolk Data Science achieves this through presentations by data science professionals and hands-on data projects for social good. By allowing beginners and experts to collaborate we accelerate learning while fostering meaningful relationships across a diverse set of backgrounds to strengthen competencies of the group as a whole. We view our efforts as a “win-win” solution because we are able to cultivate data science talent while providing data science solutions to organizations in need.

    Our Vision

    We envision a world where people from all walks of life can hone their knowledge, skills, and abilities as part of a virtual community of data science practitioners. We envision a community better served by this group committed to solving data needs through the advancement of data science technologies. We envision partnerships that spread awareness and respect for data science across disciplines and industries.

    Our Values

    Our core values guide our actions. We define these values as Respect, Responsibility, Accountability, and Service.

    • We uphold a high-degree of Respect for — fellow data scientists and partners — by actively listening, acknowledging their needs, adhering to best practices for data storage and utilization of methods and insights.
    • We take great strides to be Responsible and Accountable for the content and quality of our work.
    • We Serve others with unwavering commitment, passion, and enthusiasm.
